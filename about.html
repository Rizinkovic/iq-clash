About IQ Clash

IQ Clash is a modern quiz app designed to challenge your intellect across 10 diverse subjects. Test your knowledge in Mathematics, History, Geography, and more, with 10-second timed questions to keep you on your toes. Share your scores with friends using unique links and compete to be the smartest!

Built with passion by Rizinkovic, this project aims to make learning fun and engaging.
